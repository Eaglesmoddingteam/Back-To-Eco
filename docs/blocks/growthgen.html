Growth Generator



  • The growthgen is a smart block which can extract 10 GrP out of any kind of vanilla sapling.
    It can output GrP on the top and the bottom, and extracts from the blocks on the same y-level within a radius of one block.
The growthgen was first added in Back to Eco V1.0, it doesnt require any kind of redstone or other power source to work, just place saplings around him and, enjoy!
